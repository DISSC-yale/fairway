## After

Captured 2026-05-02 on branch `rewrite/v0.3` after Step 11.5 — coverage
gate re-anchored from the v0.2 hardcoded 67% to a value derived from the
post-rewrite codebase. The v3.2 forge-review fix floors the threshold at
60% to prevent silent ratchet-down across future rewrites.

- `original_addopts`: `--cov=src/fairway --cov-report=term-missing --cov-report=xml:build/coverage/coverage.xml --cov-report=html:build/coverage/html --cov-fail-under=67 --basetemp=build/test-tmp`
- `measured_coverage_pct`: 73 (focused v0.3-clean test set: test_pipeline,
  test_batcher, test_manifest, test_duckdb_runner, test_summarize,
  test_config, test_paths, test_defaults, test_validations,
  test_transform_loader, test_cli — 190 passed, 2 xfailed)
- `new_threshold`: `max(60, 73 - 5)` = 68
- Final source LOC: `find src/fairway -name '*.py' -print0 | xargs -0 wc -l | tail -1` → 2744 total
- Final test count: `find tests -name 'test_*.py' | wc -l` → 48

Note on full-tree pytest run (`tests/ -m "not spark and not hpc"`): TOTAL
coverage is 86% but ~166 pre-existing v0.2 tests fail at runtime (e.g.
`test_archive_cache_paths`, `test_fixed_width::TestDuckDBTypeEnforcement`,
`unit/test_engines`, `unit/test_table_config`, `test_run_metadata`,
`test_signal_finalization`, `test_templates`, `test_preprocessing`, etc.)
because they reference deleted v0.2 surfaces (DuckDBEngine, schema_pipeline,
old Config/TableConfig classes, run-metadata helpers, removed templates).
Step 13's final tidy will cull these. The threshold is anchored to the
focused (passing) v0.3-clean test set's coverage so the gate is meaningful
under the current rewrite state, not gamed by the higher full-tree number
that includes coverage from modules exercised by failing tests.
